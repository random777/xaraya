5.10. Find Out What Utility Modules Are Available

There are a number of utility modules available to carry out features that are required by many item modules within Xaraya. Examples of available utility modules are comments, ratings, and categorisation. Take a look at www.xaraya.com to find out what other utility modules are available and if they can be used in lieu of parts of the code that you would otherwise be writing for your own module.
