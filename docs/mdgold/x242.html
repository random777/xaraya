5.2. Decide on the Type of Your Module

There are two broad types of module available in Xaraya. Item modules are modules which contain their own content and operate on that content, whereas utility modules are modules which contain additional information or functionality for the content of other modules. Examples of item modules are news, FAQ, and downloads. Examples of utility modules are comments, ratings, and global index. Utility modules can either work in the same way as item modules, or they can operate through the use of hooks, which allow module functions to be acted upon without being explicitly called by other modules. Hooks are normally used for items that are not part of a piece of content but directly related to it
